About

Mar 11th, 2013

I’m a post-doc in Paris at LSCP on the Bootphon ERC project.

Previously, I did a PhD on Bayesian modeling applied to video games AI at University of Grenoble, E-Motion team at INRIA, and LPPA at Collège de France. All my Ph.D work on StarCraft (and datasets) can be found on my old website at INRIA.

I enjoy skying, sailing, and rock climbing when I can. I rarely make noise with friends. I used to be an hardcore gamer (top 20 France at Warcraft III when I was a kid), now I’m a “casual++” Dota 2 player (4.3/4.4k solo MMR). I used to hack physical stuff, now mostly software. Of course I also enjoy all the good stuff in life like wine, beer, cheese, food, traveling, music, cinema, photography, friends and family…
